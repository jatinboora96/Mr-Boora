Rishikesh


“An abode of sages”
Gallery

Description:

Rishikesh is town in the Dehradun District of Uttarakhand state in India. Total population of Rishikesh is 75,020. Rishikesh is 35 KMs far from Haridwar, and 240 KMs far from New Delhi. Rishikesh has spectacular view of jungle-clad hills. Rishikesh has also confluence of River Ganges and Chandrabhaga. Rishikesh is a holy town with so many Ashrams and it is famous for the yoga worldwide. Rishikesh is now world famous as a Yoga Capital of the world. Every year during the March month one week long International yoga festival is hosted by Rishikesh.


  • Timings :

    Rishikesh is located at an altitude of 360 meters above the sea level in the middle of mountains and surrounded by forest. Rishikesh can be visited throughout the year. November to February is winter time. April May & June is summer. July to Mid-September is rainy season here. Lowest temperature in winter is around 7 C while highest temperature in May June can go up to 43 C.

  • Places to Eat in Rishikesh:

    • Rishikesh is well known destination for Indian as well as foreign tourists, so Rishikesh has lots of varieties in food. Pani Puri, Fried Potato, Samosa, Kachori, Bhel, Chowmein, Puff, Noodles are popular snacks. In Rishikesh apart from traditional Indian cuisine Chinese, Continental, Italian, Maxican, Middle Eastern and Israeli food is available. Non vegetarian food is prohibited in Rishikesh area because it is a holy place. Some of the famous restaurants:

    1. Chotiwala: In the Swargashram area on the other side river from car parking. It is one of the old restaurants in Rishikesh and very popular among the tourist from India.
    2. Little Buddha cafe: Little buddha cafe is located in Lakshman Jhula area. Cafe has good variety in Nepali, European and Thai food. It also has good view of the river.
    3. Honey Hut: Unique concept based cafe. Honey Hut is located in Lakshman Jhula. The cafe uses honey instead of sugar in every item.
    4. Flying Folks, (11 KMs away)
    5. Madras Cafe: Near the Ram Jhula Parking. Famous for South Indian food.
    6. Other Restaurants in Rishikesh: Ganga Beach Cafe, Freedom Cafe, Ramana's Garden, Tat Cafe, Madras Cafe, Tulsi Restaurant, Flavors Restaurants, The Pyramid Cafe, Caf Okaeri, Moksha, The Dosa Cafe, Tattva Restaurant,etc.
