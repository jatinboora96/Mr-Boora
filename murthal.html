Murthal

Amrik sukhdev dhaba


“One of the most famed eatery on the Ambala-Delhi GT Road”
Gallery

Description:
Even with the name of a “dhaba” it is no less than a 3 star hotel, with great ambience and food.It offers North Indian, South Indian dishes, mouth-watering Chinese, Continental food and an array of Sweet dishes. Not only you get to eat at a great place, you also get a great fun long drive on NH-44 with your family and friends. If you are planning on a long drive and dining at a great place without spending much money, this is the place you should visit. It’s a must go place for foodies.
